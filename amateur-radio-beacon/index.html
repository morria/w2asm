Important Information

Audio Simulator

This app generates audio signals only. Connect to your transmitter's audio input for RF transmission.

License Required

Operating a radio transmitter requires an amateur radio license. Ensure compliance with local regulations.

FCC Part 97

In the US, beacon stations must comply with FCC Part 97 regulations. Know your responsibilities.
